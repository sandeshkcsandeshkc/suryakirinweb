Renewable Energy Solutions

Designed for the
Long Horizon

Solar energy is only as good as the engineering behind it. We size systems to genuine load, specify for longevity over cost, and remain on call long after commissioning — because a system that fails in year three was never truly built.

Solar Rooftop Systems

Custom load-analysis and system design for residential, institutional, and industrial premises. Every installation is engineered, not templated — from shading analysis through inverter selection to warranty-backed commissioning.

Solar Water Pumping

Variable-frequency drive and direct-coupled pumping systems engineered for mountain hydrology — reliable, low-maintenance water access for agriculture and remote communities.

System Manufacturing

In-house fabrication of galvanised mounting structures and balance-of-system components. Built to specification, climate-tested, and ready to install — removing the supply-chain uncertainty from every project.
